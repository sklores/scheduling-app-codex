Privacy Policy

Business: GCDC · Last updated: March 1, 2026

GCDC collects and processes employee phone numbers solely for operational schedule notifications, including shift schedules, shift updates, and related staffing alerts.

Information We Collect

  • Employee name
  • Employee phone number
  • Shift and schedule details needed to deliver notifications

How We Use Information

We use phone numbers only to send scheduling and shift-related SMS messages to employees. We do not use this information for marketing campaigns.

Sharing and Sales

GCDC does not sell phone numbers and does not share phone numbers with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Opt-Out

Employees may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at info@gcdcheese.com.
